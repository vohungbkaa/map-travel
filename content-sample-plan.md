# Content Sample Plan

## Muc Tieu

Tao mot bo noi dung mau that dep cho 1 khu vuc nho. Day la phan quan trong nhat de kiem chung san pham.

Muc tieu khong phai nhieu noi dung. Muc tieu la noi dung du dep de nguoi xem thay:

```text
Noi nay dang de kham pha.
```

## Khu Vuc Mau

Chon 1 trong cac loai:

- Thon/xa que nha.
- Lang nghe.
- Khu du lich cong dong.
- Khu quanh homestay.
- Pho co/khu an uong nho.
- Vung gan truong hoc/co so giao duc.

Tieu chi chon:

- Co the lay anh/video that.
- Co it nhat 10 diem dang xem.
- Co cau chuyen dia phuong.
- Co nguoi co the review thong tin.
- De di thuc dia neu can.

## Danh Sach Noi Dung Can Co

### Area

- Ten khu vuc.
- Anh cover ngang dep.
- Mo ta 150-250 chu.
- Vi tri trung tam.
- Ban kinh goi y: 3-5km.

### 10-20 Places

Moi place can:

- Ten.
- Category.
- Toa do.
- Anh cover.
- Mo ta 80-150 chu.
- 1 local tip.
- Gio nen di.
- Gia tham khao neu la quan/dich vu.
- Link Google Maps ngoai neu co.

It nhat 5 place nen co gallery 3-5 anh.

It nhat 3 place nen co video ngan 30-60 giay hoac link YouTube.

It nhat 3 place nen co audio thuyet minh 30-90 giay neu co the.

## Mau Dia Diem

### Dia Diem 1: Dinh Lang

```text
Ten: Dinh Thon E
Category: historic
Mo ta: Ngan gon ve lich su, nam xay dung, le hoi, vai tro voi cong dong.
Local tip: Nen ghe vao buoi sang, giu yen lang khi vao khu tho tu.
Media: anh mat tien, san dinh, chi tiet kien truc, video 45s.
```

### Dia Diem 2: Quan An Dia Phuong

```text
Ten: Quan banh co Hoa
Category: food
Mo ta: Mon dac trung, cau chuyen gia dinh, vi sao nguoi dia phuong hay an.
Local tip: Nen di truoc 9h vi thuong het som.
Gia: 10k-30k.
Media: anh mon an, anh chu quan, video canh lam banh.
```

### Dia Diem 3: Cafe/Viewpoint

```text
Ten: Cafe Gio Dong
Category: cafe
Mo ta: View canh dong/song/nui, khong gian, thoi diem dep.
Local tip: Nen di 16:30-18:00.
Gia: 25k-50k.
Media: anh view, anh quan, video hoang hon.
```


## Quy Trinh San Xuat Noi Dung

1. Liet ke 20 diem ung vien.
2. Chon 10 diem tot nhat cho MVP.
3. Thu thap toa do.
4. Chup anh cover cho tung diem.
5. Viet mo ta ngan.
6. Quay 3-5 video ngan cho diem noi bat.
7. Ghi audio thuyet minh neu co.
8. Nhap vao app.
9. Dua cho 5-10 nguoi xem thu.
10. Sua lai noi dung theo phan hoi.

## Tieu Chi Noi Dung Tot

- Khong viet nhu van ban hanh chinh.
- Mo ta ngan, co hinh anh cu the.
- Moi diem co ly do nen ghe.
- Co meo dia phuong thuc dung.
- Anh sang, ro, khong qua toi.
- Video ngan, vao thang chu de.
- Audio giong noi ro, khong on nen qua nhieu.
